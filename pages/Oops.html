Welcome Speech by the Principal On behalf of the management, staff and students of Faith Comprehensive High School, I welcome you to our official website. Faith Comprehensive High School is an all-gender school founded September 2012, situated at Penpe/Elekute, Owode Yewa, Ogun State.

At Faith Comprehensive High School, excellence is our watch word, we believe that whatever is worth doing at all is worth doing well, we seek to establish this standard in our every operations and instill this value in our Wards. Every boy child committed in our care is guaranteed quality education under skilled instructors. We aim to establish a solid foundation for all our Children through their academic and professional journey in life. We are also interested in the physical, social and psychological development of our Students, edging them to become responsible and impact driven members of society.


Our facilities are up-to-date making us infra-structurally positioned to deliver quality education to your children. We welcome applications from all parts of the country. We are ever ready to make Kings out of your male wards.
Connect with us via faithschools@yahoo.com. We look forward to having a rewarding relationship with you.

Welcome to our Website!

Olusegun Adisa O.
